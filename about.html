My story

How I found my way into UX.

In middle school, I was assigned to build a website for a class project. Around me, the assignment was met with the usual groans—too much work, too little fun. But I loved it. I loved deciding where things belonged, watching a page take shape, and finding that a screen could hold an idea in a way that felt entirely my own.

Soon, that class became the one I looked forward to most, the reason I was excited to go to school. While everyone else was counting down until the project was over, I was already wondering what I could make next. That small beginning was what pulled me toward design in the first place.
