The Person At The Centre Of Everything

Stacy
Keech

22 . Student . Atlanta, GA

Drugstore
Intermediate
iOS User
Her image here

Stacy wasn't invented. She's a composite of 24 research participants, young adults making purchasing decisions in a system designed to move inventory, not help them feel confident.

What she says she wants

"Better shade matching. More reviews from people who look like me."

What she actually needs

Confidence. A signal that says this one is right for you.

The gap we had to close

More information was making her more anxious, not less.

How Might We Statement

How might we help Stacy discover affordable makeup in a way that feels reliable instead of risky?
